Ni Tang (唐妮)

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the School of Informatics, Xiamen University. I received my M.S. degree in 2024 from the School of Science, Jimei University, supervised by Assoc. Prof. Dongxiao Zhang.

My research interests include, but are not limited to, computer vision (image restoration) and machine learning (unsupervised learning and continual learning). Recently, my research has focused on underwater image enhancement, diffusion models, and all-in-one image restoration.
